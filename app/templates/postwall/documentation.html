Get Started

Connect to Blockchain wallet

To start using Rosetta on Chain, you need to log into a Blockchain network with a Blockchain wallet app. At the moment, the app only works on Ropsten testnet. For wallet applications, we recommend using Metamask for stable performance as it is used in our development processes.

Metamask

Metamask is a blockchain wallet app that stores and manages your blockchain accounts, and secures your accounts by passing your account details to blockchain apps without giving out your credentials. For more information about Metamask, please visit their official site.

Setting up Metamask

To use Metamask you need to install it on your browser. Installation instructions are accessible here. If you don't have an account yet, create a blockchain account following these instructions. If you have one, read this to import your account.

Ropsten testnet

At present, Rosetta on Chain only supports Ropsten testnet, a blockchain network for testing blockchain applications. For detail about the network, visit here. Make sure you have switched to the testnet on your wallet before using Rosetta on Chain.

Getting Token

Before you can make a post on Rosetta on Chain, you must have some tokens to make transactions. To obtain tokens on Ropsten testnet, follow these instructions to get some from faucets.

Using Rosetta on Chain

Rosetta looks like a typical social network and is easy to use. Below are some brief descriptions of the main functions available at the moment.

Homepage

Viewing posts

By default, Rosetta on Chain shows all the posts made by every user. It is to maximize the opportunity of all messages to reach the public. In a later version, a focus mode will be introduced to only view followed users' content.

Writing posts

Because of the high storage cost of blockchains, the app only supports textual content at the moment. Whenever you create a post, you must confirm transaction from your blockchain wallet as uploading content counts as transactions to the blockchain. No additional cost will be incurred except the transaction fee paid to the miners.

Profile

The app provides personal profile view where you may read all the content a user has uploaded.

Viewing your profile

To access your profile, click on the "profile" button on the navigation bar. The app will show you the profile of the selected account on your wallet.

Viewing others profile

To view others' profile, click on the username on the posts on your homepage.

Creating posts

Transaction confirmation

To successfully upload a post, you must confirm a transaction from your wallet application. This is to make a request to the blockchain to process your content upload. A toll will be taken by the miners on the blockchain in order to handle your request.

Waiting for handling

Unlike traditional social networks where making posts are almost instant, blockchain networks take a relatively long time to handle any request. It could take a few minutes before your message is broadcasted. With wallets like Metamask, you may check your transaction status or speed it up by paying an extra toll.
