Welcome to the Wall!

In here, the only wall you have is the post wall where you catch up with everyone's updates on the network. No obscure policy or algorithm hidden behind the wall. It is all determined by you.

The Wall makes use of blockchain technologies to store and manipulate its data. It means that your contents posted onto the Wall are securely kept on a public network, that nobody may modify your messages.

Get Started

Using the Wall is simple and nice. Just follow the 3 steps:

1.Login to a blockchain wallet

To get started, make sure that a blockchain wallet is installed on your browser. We recommend Metamask as it is used in our development process to ensure best compatibility. For instruction on getting started with Metamask please read this. The wall will ask for permission to connect to your blockchain wallet account on start.

2.Make a post

Once logged in, all the posts will appear on your homepage and you may make use of the text box on the top to make a post.

3.Confirm transaction

Whenever you make a post, you must make a blockchain transaction via your account. Your blockchain wallet will automatically prompt you for confirmation of transaction.

Done!

Once the transaction is processed by the blockchain, your post will be visible to everyone!
